Kenya Community Development Foundation (KCDF)

Mentor Africa Network is working with KCDF in rolling out a coaching and mentoring project for secondary and primary schools in Masinga and Ndithiini regions of Machakos County. The project commenced in January 2018 and is in progress.

The specific deliverable of Mentor Africa Network include:

  • Training teacher mentors in 18 secondary schools
  • Training teacher mentors in 21 primary schools
  • Training peer mentors in 18 secondary schools and 21 primary schools
  • Developing coaching and mentorship manuals for both teacher and peer mentors
  • Supporting the roll out of the program and developing a model for upscaling and replication of the program
  • Monitoring and evaluation of the coaching and mentoring program.

    Kenyatta University

    Kenyatta University directorate of mentoring has engaged Mentor Africa Network in providing training of both the faculty and staff mentors. The working relationship will ensure that Mentor Africa Network will provide coaching and mentoring technical support especially in training both staff and peer mentors. The recent faculty and staff mentors training got 133 mentors trained.

    Deliverance Church Upper Kabate (DC UK)

    The partnership between the two organizations will see the church youth at DC UK taken through a 4 month Mentor Africa Network mentoring program. For sustainability of the program, Mentor Africa Network has trained mentors who are bone fide members of DC UK. These mentors are practitioners from different professions ranging from HR practitioners, lecturers, administrators, NGO staff and experts from other areas of specialization. Mentor Africa will provide technical support during the 4 months mentoring period.
