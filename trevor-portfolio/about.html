Trevor Muchiri is a Nairobi based practicing contemporary architecture and infrastructure.” Trevor studied architecture at the University of Nairobi. Trevor's skills range widely in its practice, with his architecture portfolio showcasing structures and product design as well as designs for residences and large-scale infrastructure.

Sustainability is a focus for Trevor Muchiri, whose architecture portfolio showcases designs for homes as well as commercial spaces. Over the years, he has worked on residential, commercial, hotel, public, and research facilities, as well as many more projects.
