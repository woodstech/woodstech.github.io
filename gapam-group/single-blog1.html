Why your workplace should be considering a full time office cleaner during COVID-19

  • By Paul Gachoka
  • 06 Likes
  • 02 Comments

If you have employees in the office – even if it’s a skeleton staff – you have an obligation to provide a safe working environment, and a full time office cleaner can help you meet your responsibilities as an employer.

” Always check the latest government advice – and be aware that this article and links contain general and non-binding advice. Employers should consider the specific conditions of your workplace and comply with all applicable legislation, including the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974. ”
Jessica Mcdade

Not all businesses will need a full time office cleaner – but at the very least, you should be considering increasing the frequency of your cleaning to daily office cleaning in the morning and/or evening and working with a reputable office cleaning company. As well as a full time office cleaner, we can provide a daytime office janitor for a set period during office hours – from as little as two hours per day – to help clean and maintain office hygiene.
