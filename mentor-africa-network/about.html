About Us

Mentor Africa Network brings together a network of mentors who walk the protégé through a structured mentoring journey with a drive to help them navigate and connect to their purpose in life.

Who we are

We are a mentoring organization providing coaching and mentorship solutions to the youth of Africa. We equip and empower the youth with requisite life skills to choose, move and respond to their purpose in life. Our programs target the youth of Africa with a purpose of empowering them to fully exploit their God given potential and create new possibilities for their lives. Mentor Africa Network brings together a network of mentors who walk the protégé through a structured mentoring journey with a drive to help them navigate and connect to their purpose in life.

Our Story

Our mission is to nurture the lives of African youth by providing a network of coaching and mentorship opportunities and industry linkages for holistic transformation

We have a Vision to create new possibilities for the youth of Africa through coaching and mentoring

Motto: Transformation through Mentorship

Our Core Values

Unlocking full potential

We are proponents and crusaders of youth performing and living at their best! Unleashing the super powers of their mind and altering their limiting realities.

Self-discovery

We support the youth to be self-aware, identify their unique talents and harness their exceptional capabilities for prosperity. Self-discovery makes people become truly who they are!

Talent development

Undeveloped talent is like unfulfilled promise. We help nurture and promote the natural and God given gifts and talents. The success and fruitfulness of human beings is linked to the discovery of their seed; their talent.
