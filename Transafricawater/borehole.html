DAB PUMPS

Divertron automatic submersible Pressure Pumps are specifically designed for pressurised water supply in domestic and other small scale applications. They are for convenient for in-tank installation with the feature of a jacketed motor for improved cooling. They include the unique feature of an integral electronic controller which regulates the pump operation and ensures constant supply pressure and flow. The controller features an in-built flow sensor and air vessel which automatically starts the pump on demand and stops it as flow reduces. Dry run and motor overload protection is also provided.
