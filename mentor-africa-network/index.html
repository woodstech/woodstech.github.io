Mentor Africa
Network

We are a mentoring organization providing coaching and mentorship solutions to the youth of Africa. Mentor Africa Network brings together a network of mentors who walk the protégé through a structured mentoring journey.

Trusted by clients across Africa whom we have worked with

Support

We support the youth to be self-aware, identify their unique talents and harness their exceptional capabilities for prosperity. It helps people become truly who they are.

Performance

We help nurture the God given gifts hence, the success and fruitfulness of human beings is linked to the discovery of their talent.

Manage

We are proponents and crusaders of youth performing and living at their best! Unleashing the super powers of their mind and altering their limiting realities.

Mentorship

Become a mentee

As a mentee, one of your main priorities is to be open to learning. Some of the benefits include:

  • Exposure to new ideas and ways of thinking
  • Advice on developing strengths and overcoming weaknesses
  • Increase your social and academic confidence
  • Develop your communication, study and personal skills
  • Exposure to a network of friends and professionals
